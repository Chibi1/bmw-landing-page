The Brand New 2018 BMW M3 Sedan




TOO POWERFUL.

An uncompromising four-door high-performance sports car of breathtaking design. With an array of motorsport-inspired innovations. For even greater dynamism and even more agile handling. For adrenalin-steeped driving moments where one thing instantly becomes clear: this is about setting personal bests – the BMW M3 Sedan.

PUSH THE LIMITS.

You can tell that the new BMW M3 Sedan is still a genuine athlete away from the racetrack. It always exhibits extremely precise, agile handling – above all in demanding driving situations. The powerful M engine lays down its thrust in precisely measured doses onto the road, using the manual six-speed M transmission or the seven-speed double-clutch automatic transmission.

COMPETITION PACKAGE.

Thanks to an enhanced engine output and specially tuned Adaptive M suspension, the M3 Competition Sedan offers a noticeable increase in driving performance. The M sports exhaust system with black, chrome-plated twin exhaust pipes creates a powerful engine sound. The package also includes M lightweight sport seats and exclusive 20" M light star alloy wheels with mixed tyres.
